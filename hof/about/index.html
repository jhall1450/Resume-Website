About

Our History

Image provided by Kaboompics

House of Flavors, opened in 2003, has been known as the restaurant to visit if you're in Loveland. The owner, Paul Overton, had a dream to give the good people of Loveland great food for a great price, and he did just that. Over the years House of Flavors has gone from nobody, to being on the roadmap of anyone visiting Cincinnati because of how much of an icon it has become. We wouldn't be here today if it weren't for our loyal customers, so to that we thank you!
