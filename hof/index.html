Something for Everyone

Image provided by Lucas

  • Lunch

    Whether you're out for your lunch break or just need a quick bite to eat, House of Flavors is the perfect stop for lunch. Come on in and grab a sandwich, salad, or soup and you'll leave happy. Lunch is available from Opening to 2pm.

  • Dinner

    House of Flavors has a diverse list of entrees that you and the family will love! If your can't decide on what sounds good, stop on down for a little bit of everything. Dinner available from 2pm to Close.

  • Drinks

    We've got a diverse list of beer, mixed drinks, liquor, or even wine that will fit anyones palette. Our critically aclaimed bar will fit just about anyones style, so stop on by for a quick drink! Happy Hour runs from 5pm to 7pm, and drinks are $1.50 off.
